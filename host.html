Know Your People

Queue’d Hosting exists for one reason: make music and user management easy for small businesses.

See your patron’s activity levels, how long they’ve been ‘checked in’, and even send them push messages directly to their smartphone.

Earn Extra Cash

Users have the option to purchase vote packages that they can use in your queue. When they purchase anything while checked in to your venue, you get 30% of whatever we make.

Our system costs you nothing to use - in fact, we aim to make you money in the process.

Democratize Your Music

Our core feature, a crowd sourced queue, allows you to ‘set and forget’ your music management. Your patrons hear music they collectively decide on, and you don’t have to worry about a DJ.

Running your entire venue — analytics, user management, music and all — can be done from a single device.

Give it a Try.

It’s free to use, free to download, and you don’t even have to sign in to anything!
